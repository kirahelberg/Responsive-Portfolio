About

KIRA HELBERG is a Senior Analyst at Flywheel Partners, a Healthcare Communications Agency. She graduated in 2016 from UCLA with a B.A. in Economics. Currently, she lives in New York City.


She has always been curious about web development, and finally started her journey in January 2019 at the Columbia University Engineering Coding Bootcamp program.


Besides coding, her interests include: drawing, painting, design, photography, cooking, baking, and listening to true-crime podcasts (she's a Murderino!).
